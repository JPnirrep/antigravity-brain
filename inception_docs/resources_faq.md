How do I get higher rate limits?

Reach out to our support team with details about your use case, expected
volume, and any latency or throughput requirements. We’ll review your needs
and can raise your limits accordingly.

How do I handle rate limits?

Implement exponential backoff in your client code. When you receive a rate
limit error (429) or a server error (503), wait before retrying.

How are tokens counted?

Tokens are pieces of text that our models process. A token is roughly 4
characters for English text. Both input and output tokens count toward your
usage limits.
